Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

TL;DR: We respect your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for the game to function and improve. Your personal information is never sold to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Under Attack ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Under Attack (the "Game").

By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the Game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Game, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Game Usage Data: Gameplay statistics, progress, achievements, high scores
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics
  • Analytics Data: Session duration, feature usage, in-game events (via Amplitude Analytics)

2.2 Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and save data
  • User preferences and settings
  • Unlocked achievements and upgrades
  • Tutorial completion status

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless voluntarily provided for support
  • Precise location data
  • Access to your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To provide core game features and save your progress
  • Improvements: To analyze gameplay patterns and improve game balance and features
  • Bug Fixes: To identify and fix technical issues and crashes
  • Analytics: To understand how players interact with the game (using Amplitude Analytics)
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google Mobile Ads
  • Purchases: To process in-app purchases and subscriptions through RevenueCat

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • Data transmitted to our analytics services is encrypted
  • We do not store personal information on our servers
  • We regularly update our security practices

Note: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Under Attack is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Advertising and Tracking

7.1 Advertisements

The Game displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. These ads may be personalized based on your interests. Ad networks may use tracking technologies to collect information about your device and usage.

7.2 Opt-Out Options

You can limit ad tracking on your device:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to the information we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (note: most data is stored locally on your device)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics or personalized advertising
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Data Retention

  • Local game data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics data is retained according to our third-party service providers' policies
  • Support correspondence is retained for up to 2 years

10. International Users

If you are accessing the Game from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the Game
  • Sending a notification through the Game (for material changes)

Your continued use of the Game after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
